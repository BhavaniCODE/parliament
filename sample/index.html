About Us

The course on Indian Parliamentary democracy attempts to trace the theoretical and practical linkages between the Constitution of India, the Parliament, the Judiciary and the civil society. Democracy is known as the system of checks and balance. While analyzing the synergies between the Legislature, the Judiciary and the Civil Society, the course will also highlight the points of divergence and constant tension, which involves the interpretation of Constitutional provisions. The four institutions mentioned above are not viewed as independent but interdependent entities, which have to function synergistically for a constitutional republic to be successful.
